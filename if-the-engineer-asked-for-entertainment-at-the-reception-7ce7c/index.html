“HAPPINESS SCOUTER”

If you send a photo via LINE, it will recognize the facial expression of the person in the picture and measure the “happy fighting power”**! ! something like. On the list page, it is displayed in the ranking format of happy fighting power.

Entertainment and results

  • Brief introduction and explanation while showing slides on the screen.
  • Measure for 5 minutes and have the photograph taken on the spot be measured. The list page is displayed on the screen and is live.
  • Finally, check the result and give a prize to the person who took the photo with the best happy fighting power.
  • Keep it free to use after entertainment. (During the second party)

I decided to participate freely because I need to be friends with my LINE account, 48 of 66 guests participated.

In the 5 minutes of entertainment, I was able to collect about 80 photos and finally 150 photos. I also gathered unrelated photos… lol

Where improvement is needed

  • How to score The results were often negative because the emotions other than “happiness” and “calm” were calculated negatively. It was a reception, so I had to think a little more. ..

  • Accuracy of target face As shown below, even people in the back who had nothing to do with it were recognized and affected the score. Can you target by the size of the bounding box?
