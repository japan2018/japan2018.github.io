#kaggle’s say depends on status

Users are ranked in kaggle. It is called Progression. It’s a progress level or something like a title.

Depending on this rank, the degree of attention to the posted script and the degree of influence of the statement differ.

When you register, you will be given the title novice, and if you meet certain conditions, you will become Contributor, If you want to become a higher rank from there, you must make statements that collect good evaluations from others, obtain medals, etc.

kaggle is a competitive competition, so if you just collect medals, it is better to read the trial results of others and improve the accuracy without posting your answer until the last minute. That alone does not make kaggle prosper. kaggle is also a place for technical exchange. Contribute to your prosperity by posting your own answers and commentary about your answers to prosper. By posting useful information, you can get a good reputation and improve your rank.

First of all, if you register as a beginner novice is boring, so let’s aim at Contributor. I have summarized the method of becoming a Contributor, as easy as possible with a diagram.
