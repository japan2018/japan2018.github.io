How to manage a reading party

Holding time

Late April to mid-May 2020. Due to the influence of COVID-19, it was held during the time when everyone was teleworking from home.

Holding cycle

It was held once a week and every Thursday from 13:00 to 14:00 for an hour. It was held 5 times in total.

How to divide 5 times

1st: chap1～chap8 The second: chap9-chap21 The third: chap22-chap37 The fourth: chap38-chap57 The fifth: chap58-chap67

About 50 to 100 pages per week was the range.

Number of participating members

This time, 6 people (including myself) will be able to do so due to their time.

Preparation by the day

Members of the reading group will read the next event range and select two sections that they like.

And in the file shared by everyone, *** Two favorite parts and their words** And ***Why did you choose that part? How do you relate to your business? Why I liked it**

I had you write. It’s easy to say about 2-3 lines.

Operation on the day

The reading party was held online at Teams.

For the first time, I will carry out the operation on the day, From the second time onward, we asked two people, who would like to become leaders, to alternate weekly.

The operator shares the “2 favorite parts and the reason” that everyone wrote down.

The person who wrote the contents displayed on the screen,

Read out what you liked and why you gave it We asked him to talk about his/her past work, future thoughts, and what he/she usually thinks.

Although it is a few lines in the memo, it is like feeling more detailed.

And other participants added their own thoughts and experiences to the place and the reasons given, Inflate the story

It is the flow.

Doing this for 5 people, I just talked a little at the end and it was like an hour.

Impressions implemented

I think that each participating member has their own thoughts, but I

  1. Everybody was interested in different parts, and the different parts came from their different projects and development experiences, and it was fun to hear various opinions.

  2. On the contrary, the important points that many members liked are the points where the non-verbalized information was clearly expressed, such as “What do we all want to value?” Became

  3. It was a reading event in which not only the people who actually do things close to the leaders and PdM, but also the members who follow them participated. By learning while sharing product management, I think that the members have been able to smoothly follow the leaders and that the leaders can smoothly lead the development.

  4. The reading party was just after completing a large development project, so I was able to deepen my reflection.

Product management is completely impossible with just one book reading party, I think it was good that everyone, including myself, went one step ahead.
