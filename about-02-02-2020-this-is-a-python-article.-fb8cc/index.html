Investigation

Constraint

This time, the year has 4 digits and the month and day have 2 digits. Therefore, the year, month, and day that are out of digits are filled with zeros (0). (Example: “1 year” → “0001 year”, “January” → “January”) Also, the target year/month/day is from 01/01/0001 to 31/12/9999.

Program

from collections import Counter

result

The target number of days this time was 3652059 days from January 01, 0001 to December 31, 9999. Among them, the date consisting of two or less numbers such as February 02, 2020 was 928 day.
