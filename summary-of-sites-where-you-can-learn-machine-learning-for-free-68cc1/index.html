Chainer tutorial

https://tutorials.chainer.org/ja/

For those who don’t hesitate what to learn from, this is the site for the time being Popular because it is written in Japanese and easy to understand

Medical AI specialized course online lecture materials

https://japan-medical-ai.github.io/medical-ai-course-materials/#

Also released by Chainer. It is attractive that you can use actual data. For a little application.

“DL4US,” an educational program for engineers under the supervision of the Matsuo Laboratory of the University of Tokyo

https://weblab.t.u-tokyo.ac.jp/dl4us/

You can learn a wide range from image classification to natural language processing, reinforcement learning, and GAN. The library is Keras.
