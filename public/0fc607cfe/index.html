Mar 27, 2020 Python DeepLearning AI PyTorch 

In this article, I will introduce the working style of Ogawa, who belongs to the development team of the AI Technology Department.

This article is an introduction for me.

The way you work depends on the member, and also on the family structure. I am married but I have no children.

Also, different departments work differently.

In this article, I will introduce the working week for me (Yutaro Ogawa) of AI Technology Department.

Why don’t you change jobs from your current company?

Occasionally, “Why does Ogawa work at Dentsu International Information Service (ISID)? Why not change jobs?” I was asked,

One of the main reasons I haven’t changed my job at the moment is

“An environment where ISID is very easy to work, and it is easy to balance work and private (self-study)”

is.

I will introduce the actual situation.

##“basic rule” First of all, the basic rules of the company,

  1. Scheduled working time is 7 hours
  2. Regular standard working hours are from 9:30 to 17:30 (1 hour break included)

is.

Most companies have a standard working time of 8 hours (7 hours 45 minutes), but ISID is 7 hours.

Compared to 8 hours, I work less than 1 hour every day.

If it is 1 hour x 20 days/month, it will be 20 hours less per month.

Image-wise,

It feels like “more holidays every month, about 3 days compared to an 8 hour company”.

By that time, I can take time to study various things outside working hours, I like this rule of working hours because it is easy for me to grow.

Overtime hours are Looking at openwork (formerly vorkers), it says 37 hours a month, https://www.vorkers.com/company.php?m_id=a0910000000GVZZ

According to the official figures, the company-wide average is 25.5 hours/month (FY2018 actual). (ISID official https://www.isid.co.jp/neo-pro/faq/)

My overtime hours are basically about 25 to 30 hours.

Also, since overtime hours are calculated monthly,

For example, Work 9 hours on Monday (2 hours overtime), On Tuesdays, I work two hours overtime the previous day, work short hours, work five hours, and return. (If possible due to work schedule)

“Flex system”

As mentioned earlier, the standard working time is 7 hours, but there is only the core time due to the flex system. Core time varies by department.

The core time of my AI Technology Department is 9:30-12:00. Therefore, basically, everyone comes to work at 9:30 and returns from around 17:30 to 18:30.

In the case of the department that entered when I joined the company, the core time was 13:00-15:30, Many people came from 11 o’clock or noon (that’s why everyone worked late into the night)

In my case, it’s morning type so that people around me call me “Grandpa”.

Basically, I work from 7:30 in the morning, work for 8 hours with 7 hours + 1 hour for a break, and leave the company at 15:30.

(When I finished work at 15:30 and made a phone call to my parents’ house on my way home, Parents worry that “Are you really working?”

“Telework”

Basically, you can get 2 days of telework a week. If you want to telework more than 3 days a week, you can do it with the permission of your supervisor.

Basically, I do telework 2 days a week.

Also, when the week with high load continues, In the next week, I will work from home for the entire week, and sometimes I will work while resting my body.

In the case of teleworking, the question is, “How do you handle internal meetings and meetings with customers?”

First, there is a “Regular Meeting” every week.

The regular meetings of the department are basically telework participants. The manager then shares the meeting materials with everyone on the screen and advances the meeting.

In the first place, since there are members of the Kansai branch office, the telephone call is the basic. Therefore, there is no problem in teleworking.

In-house meetings are the same.

In addition, in the case of meetings with customers, ・A certain degree of relationship ・Movement time is a waste for each other ・The quality of the meeting output does not deteriorate even if you are not in the actual place.

In that case, basically, we will hold meetings, report on progress, and give consultations via telecan.

However, in-house meetings, customer meetings, ・When I need to facilitate ・When it is very important ・If the quality of the output of the meeting is better if you are real Is I usually attend a meeting, not a telephone call.

To the end, it is a culture that “if you can use a telephone, the efficiency and the quality of the conference will not deteriorate.”

Finally, my way of working (Ogawa)

Recently (March 20th), there are many team product development and customer consulting projects.

Telework on Monday and Friday, Team up on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays for team development It is a style.
