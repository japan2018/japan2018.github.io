Below are some of the things I have learned while learning by myself. ~~ There was only one~~

1. Understand what you don’t understand.

The worst thing about self-study is that I don’t know what I’m doing. It is not good to get motivated, so let’s understand why you do not know why and what you do not understand, and take measures.

One also says. “** Clarify why you don’t understand **” and “** Take measures **”. I will say it again. “**Why**” Don’t know, where you don’t know, and “**Countermeasure**”. I will say it again. “**Cause**” and “**Countermeasure**”.

If you don’t understand and want to start screaming, please remember “Cause” and “Countermeasure”.

There are two steps to finding the cause. Find out if you don’t know “where” and where you no longer know. “Why” Write in a sentence why you don’t know. only this. Along with programming error resolution.

There are various measures. I feel awkward if I can see when I’m sticking, when I use another teaching material, when I go back, and when I skip it. In fact, if I understand the cause, I feel that I will decide which measure I want to take.

Especially, if you don’t know mathematical symbols or mathematical formulas, it’s easy to fall down, so if you don’t understand it, let’s leave it alone for another study. It doesn’t matter if you overdo it.
