Q2. What is Jamaican How to play?

A2. White dice x 5 numbers are used once each to perform The four arithmetic operations, and the sum of the black dice x 2 numbers is created.

For example, if the white die roll is (1,2,3,5,5) and the black die roll is (10,6), You can add all the rolls of the white dice to make the equation 1+2+3+5+5=10+6.

■ *** White dice x 5 numbers can be used only once each. *** ➡︎ For example, if the white die roll is (2,4,5,3,3), what can be used for calculation? 2 once, 3 twice, 4 once, 5 once. Do not use any more. (It is useless to use 2 twice or 3 three times.) ➡︎ Of course, it is not legal to not use the specified number of times. (In the above example, it is useless to use 3 only once.)

  • The four arithmetic operations are addition, subtraction, multiplication and division. *** ➡︎ “Addition” is “addition”, that is, “addition”. 　For example, calculate the sum (result of addition) such as 2+3=5. ➡︎ *“Reduction”** is “subtraction”, that is, “subtraction”**. 　For example, calculate the difference such as 5-2=3 (result of subtraction). ➡︎ **“multiplication”** is “multiplication”, that is, “multiplication”**. 　For example, calculate a product such as 3×4=12 (the result of multiplication). ➡︎ **“Division”** is “division”, that is, “division”**. 　For example, calculate a quotient such as 4÷2=2 (the result of division*).
  • There are rules that allow only divisible operations, that is, rules that do not allow irreducible fractions, and rules that also allow irreducible fractions. [1] ** In the case of rules that do not allow things that become irreducible fractions ** When the white dice roll (2,4,6,3,5), 6÷3=2 can be calculated, but The result of 6÷4=3/2 cannot be used. [2] ** In the case of rules that allow even irreducible fractions ** When the white dice roll (2,4,6,3,5), you can not only calculate 6÷3=2, The result of 6/4=3/2 is also available.
  • ** Some dice rolls can only be solved by using irreducible fractions. ** Example) When the white dice roll (2,2,4,4,6) is black die roll (30,3) The equation {(2- 2/4) + 4} × 6 = 30 + 3 holds.

■ ***The four arithmetic operations can be performed in any order. *** ➡︎ You can add two numbers first and multiply by another number in any order. In other words, you can use parentheses any number of times, anywhere. Example) When the white die roll (3,6,4,4,1) is compared to the black die roll (20,6) The equation {(3 + 6) + (4 × 4)} + 1 = 20 + 6 holds.
