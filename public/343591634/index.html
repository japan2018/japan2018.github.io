1. Logical thinking ability to see data correctly and its study method

What is the logical thinking ability to see data correctly?

This is fairly simple. And the impression is that there are many people who think they are doing it themselves and are not doing it unexpectedly.

For example, when analyzing whether taking action A in the app contributes to the continuation rate,

  1. Ratio of “People who took the action A and continued” and “People who did not do it”
  2. Percentage of “People who took action A among those who continued” and “People who did not do it”
  3. Ratio of “People who did not act A and continued” and “People who did not”

These meanings are, of course, different, but what indicators are important in your analysis? What kind of value will these be, what kind of analysis will you take, and what kind of measures will you take next?

In the example below, 1 “Ratio of those who continued the action of A” was sufficiently large ** Despite the fact that the proportions of “People who took the action of A among those who continued” and “People who did not take it” of 2 were almost the same, Because the ratio of “the number of those who did not take the action of A that continued” of 3 is large, It may be better not to let A act.

It is still simple if there is only one action called A, but if you analyze whether you also performed B action at the same time, it will suddenly become complicated. Is it possible to understand the situation and think of ways to improve it without confusion?

Importance of product development and experiment planning in anticipation of data analysis

From now on, BI tools will become more and more convenient and there will be almost nothing that cannot be done. In fact, anyone can do advanced analysis such as machine learning.

Furthermore, basic data analysis will be automated, and tools will find key indicators.

However, what is really “important” and difficult is what kind of hypothesis should be analyzed in the future, what kind of suggestions should be obtained from that, and what kind of measures should be taken. **Product design and experiment **Think about it during the planning stage.

The reason why we decided to introduce the new dashboard “Looker” before the product was released This article describes that Merpey had a data analysis platform in place before the product was released.

In fact, if you do not think about data analysis at the ** product design and experiment planning stage, you may not be able to get the data you want or the analysis results will be unreliable. .. For example, what kind of log is needed to analyze the desired data? Even if you do an AB test, you need to think about how long, how many people, and how to conduct it.

It will take some more time to be automated up to this point.

That is why, in the modern democratization of data, we believe that it is truly necessary to have an “analyzer” and a “designer” who know not only the basic physical strengths of data analysis and business but also data collection and product development. ..
