Impressions of actually taking the test

The actual exam is 20 questions in order of math, programming, and AI. The first mathematics primarily requires knowledge of linear algebra. At first, it may be annoying, but since it is a test that you do not have time to think carefully about 60 questions in 60 minutes and solve it, it is necessary to cleanly postpone calculation problems that may take time or throw it away Will be. In programming problems, there were scikit-learn in the range, so it was given at a fairly high rate. The AI part was similar to the official teaching material. (I love the gradient descent method) E It may be because it is a qualification that is conscious of test preparation, but as I mentioned in the test page, I strongly got the impression that it is an implementation and reasoning-oriented test.
