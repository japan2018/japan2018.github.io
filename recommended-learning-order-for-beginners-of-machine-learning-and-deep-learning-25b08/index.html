Recommended learning order for beginners of machine learning and deep learning

Dec 10, 2019 Python machine learning deep learning AI machine learning introduction 

# Target of this article

Purpose of writing the article

The words AI and deep learning have been around for a long time, but I don’t think there are many people who really understand their meaning. I think it’s very dangerous to have a debate about what AI can do, as in Japan today.

I think the reason why there is such a debate about AI in this way is that you do not fully understand the mathematical basis of machine learning.

As long as you understand the basic theory, you should be able to easily predict what AI can do and how it will progress in the future.

Target audience & knowledge that you should have to learn machine learning

This article is for anyone who often hears AI but doesn’t really understand it.

Machine learning, especially deep learning, is fully understandable if you have a high school mathematics knowledge. However, I think it’s better to understand differentiation.

In addition, I think you should have some programming knowledge. For the books and video materials that we will introduce from now on, the basic method of studying iron plate is to learn machine learning while moving hands through programming.

So, the summary of the knowledge level of this article is that “you should be able to read programming, assuming almost no mathematical knowledge.”

Study method

Study method overview

As a learning method for machine learning, we strongly recommend learning from deep learning. In the world, deep learning seems to be treated like the boss of machine learning, but in terms of ease of understanding, that is not the case.

Certainly, with the advent of deep learning, the current AI boom has taken place. However, among the many machine learning algorithms, the neural network that is the basis of deep learning is quite simple.

Deep Learning was revolutionary,

  1. Increase the number of calculations in the neural network (I am vaguely saying lol) (make it deep),
  2. In addition to the neural network, with a little “ingenuity”, It was an explosive precision.

A little “ingenuity” of 2 is important in deep learning, but this “ingenuity” is different between (1) multidimensional data such as image recognition and (2) time series data such as natural language processing. I will.

If you can understand up to this point, you can say that you understand the basics of deep learning.

To study machine learning in general, it is enough to study deep learning. First of all, if you can understand deep learning with a lasting sense, it seems like “machine learning is easy” lol In addition, the future learning of machine learning is mainly trying to understand each algorithm and actually using it, so it may be unnecessary if you are not an engineer.

The following are the presentation materials for deep learning that I have announced at study sessions. I hope you will take a look at the books and videos I will introduce in the future to deepen your understanding.
