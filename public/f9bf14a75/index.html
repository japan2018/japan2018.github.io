Data Scientist = “Multilingual” theory

I’ve always wondered if the concept of a data scientist might be a bit closer to a “multilingual translator.” Data scientists must be able to manipulate different languages and translate between languages.

The term “language” used here means

・Business language ・Number/KPI ・Statistics/mathematical formula ・Programming language

Etc. Please think and see.

Business owners and planning departments often speak in the business language and often cannot communicate in other languages. “Aim to increase sales Yo” is not mentioned as a concrete formula or algorithm. ： ―― Although statisticians are good at dealing with difficult mathematical formulas, they are not good at talking like real business events. There is a “data scientist” to overcome this situation, which is the image of a data scientist I would like to discuss in this article. Analysis value chain and “reading” “writing”

The people involved in the value chain of the analysis process in business have the following structure. And this value chain will form a V-shaped flow by the reciprocating process of “writing” and “reading”. ** Let’s explain one by one. ** ――

First of all, most analysis processes start with words such as “our sales structure should be more visible Yo” of business managers (managers, product managers, business managers, etc.) Eventually, it will reach the computing resources through the people involved. (However, it is not the case that each person is divided and shared, and it is quite possible that one person covers multiple areas.)

Very strong skills are needed to go down this process. In general, the process of “writing” is by far more difficult than “reading”.

Planning/Management person

Business knowledge and logic must be mobilized to design meaningful, compelling and computable KPIs. It is also important that the content meets the expectations of business managers.

Analytical/statistical person

It is necessary to consider concrete measures for how to analyze and quantify the KPI you want to see. It is necessary to scrutinize the usable data, determine the range of actually used data (excluding bad data, etc.), think about the granularity of observation, and design a statistical model if necessary. You must also consider what kind of chart the results will be displayed in.

Engineer

Implement the calculation logic that the analyst thought of. It is necessary to pay attention to the processing speed by making a general-purpose design in consideration of changes in the range and granularity of data. In some cases, knowledge of statistical model packages is also required. It is also desirable that the output format is carefully considered and, if necessary, familiar with techniques such as visualization.

Computing infrastructure

Depending on the amount of data, it may be necessary to have human resources with infrastructure engineer knowledge for tuning computing resources and parallelization.

―― Basically, it is almost impossible or almost impossible to ask all of these skills from one person. Therefore, if a person who has a full stack of “writing” skills is defined as a “data scientist,” this occupation will be jammed ** in no time.
